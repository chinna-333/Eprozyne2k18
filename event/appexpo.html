Rules
  • Maximum two participants per team is allowed.
  • Each team has to send the soft copy of App/Project abstract to eprozyne2k18.app@gmail.com with subject as AppExpo
  • The abstract must pertain to mentioned format. (Download)
  • Deadline for sending your entries :03-03-2018.
  • Intimation of shortlisted teams: 04-03-07
  • Guidelines for shortlisted App / Projects:
    1. Shortlisted team must register in eprozyne2k18 website by paying the registration amount of INR 300 /-, online or at the college(spot registration) which confirms your participation in EPROZYNE 2K18.
    2. The shortlisted team should present a PPT and Working Model of App / Project in their own laptops or Computers provided by Event Authority.
    3. Maximum 10 minutes time is allotted for each team.
    4. Shortlisted participants should bring their college ID Card.
    5. Participation Certificate will be issued for all registered teams.
  • Prizes: Cash Prizes worth of INR 7,000/- will be given
