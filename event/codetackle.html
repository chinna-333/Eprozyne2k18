#Code_Tackle
#Code_tackle is an online programming contest held here at pec to unleash your coding skills to the core. Coding enthusiasts, take notice, this is a place where you would want to be if you want to flaunt your coding skills. The contest mainly focuses on programming, data structures and algorithms.
Rules
  • #Code_Tackle consists of 3 rounds.
  • It is individual event. No groups allowed
  • Problems can be solved using any language of your choice among C /C++ /Java /Python
  • No references will be provided during contest, any such activity may cause disqualification from the event.
  • Every individual must register in eprozyne2k18 website by paying registration amount of INR 150/- by 03-03-18 05:00 P.M
  • Update 03-03-18: Round 1 started (Link to Contest)
  • Round 1 will be an online round (Internet) open from 03-03-2018 8:30 PM to 04-03-2018 8:30 PM and Round 2, 3 will be conducted in our Computer Labs. (Intranet) on 06-03-2018
  • Round 2 duration is 45 minutes and Round 3 duration is 60 minutes.
  • Participants who are shortlisted from Round 1 are only eligible for Round2, and participants who are shortlisted from Round 2 are eligible for Round3.
  • All Participants should carry their college ID Cards for Round 2 and Round 3.
  • Prizes: Cash Prizes worth of INR 6,000/- will be given
