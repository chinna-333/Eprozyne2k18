Campus Ambassador Description.
Instructions to Campus Ambassadors
  • Campus Ambassador must register for atleast one event in Eprozyne2k18.
  • Only one Campus Ambassador will be selected from a college.
  • Campus Ambassadors must promote Eprozyne2k18 in their respective colleges.
  • when a student from your college, participating in Eprozyne2k18 selects Campus Ambassador option in "How did you hear about Eprozyne2k18" during their registration, it will be counted and there will be benefits depending on the count as stated below.
Benifits to Campus Ambassadors
  • Silver Star(5 or more students)
    • One event participation free
    • Eprozyne2k18 T-shirt
    • Cerificate
  • Gold Star(10 or more students)
    • Three events participation free
    • Eprozyne2k18 T-shirt
    • Cerificate
  • Double Gold Star(15 or more students)
    • All events participation free
    • Eprozyne2k18 T-shirt
    • Cerificate
