Idea Bucket
Idea Bucket is an innovative and inventive platform that serves as the foundation to many young and budding entrepreneurs such as yourselves. Idea Bucket provides an opportunity to showcase your ideas and develop your idea into reality. Come present your ideas at Eprozyne 2k18 and rediscover yourself in a unique way.
Rules
  • Maximum 3 participants per team is allowed.
  • Each team must target on single idea for starting a start-up.
  • Each team has to send the soft copy of abstract to eprozyne2k18.ib@gmail.com with subject as Idea Bucket
  • The abstract must not contain more than 500 words and pertain to be mentioned format. (Download)
  • Deadline for sending your entries :03-03-2018.
  • Intimation of shortlisted teams: 04-03-2018
  • Guidelines for shortlisted Ideas:
    1. Shortlisted team must register in eprozyne2k18 website by paying the registration amount of INR 300 /-, on or before 04-03-2018 which confirms your participation in EPROZYNE 2K18.
    2. The shortlisted team should present a PPT on their business model.
    3. Maximum 10 minutes time is allotted for each team
    4. Shortlisted participants should bring their college ID Card.
    5. Participation Certificate will be issued for all register teams.
    6. The candidates are expected to present themselves in proper apparel and decorum.
  • Prizes: Cash Prizes worth of INR 6,000/- will be given
