Techno Talk (PPT)
Paper presentation is an effective tool to aid learning. Eprozyne is aimed at inviting Paper Presentations in the field of, primarily, Computer Science and Information Technology and, generally, all interdisciplinary streams of Engineering Sciences, having central focus on sustainable computing applications, which may be of some use in enhancing the quality of life and contribute effectively to realize the nations vision of sustainable inclusive developments. Eprozyne is providing an opportunity to showcase your presentation skills in a unique and massive platform.
Rules
  • Maximum two participants per team is allowed.
  • Each team has to send the soft copy of abstract paper to eprozyne2k18.ppt@gmail.com with subject as TechnoTalk
  • All papers must be in IEEE format. (No other formats are encouraged)
  • Deadline for sending your paper :04-03-2018 9:00 P.M.
  • Intimation of shortlisted paper: 05-03-2018
  • Guide lines for Shortlisted Papers:
    1. Shortlisted team must register in eprozyne2k18 website by paying the registration amount of INR 300/-, online which confirms your participation in EPROZYNE 2K18 or at the college(spot registration)
    2. Number of slides of your presentation should be limited to 15 slides.
    3. Maximum 10 minutes time is allotted for each team.
    4. Shortlisted participants should bring their college ID Card.
    5. Participation Certificate will be issued for all registered teams.
  • Prizes: Cash Prizes worth of INR 10,000/- will be given
