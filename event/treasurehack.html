<Treasure_Hack>
Treasure Hack is an amazing event to show off your quick thinking and problem solving skills. This contest mainly focuces on your thinking skills and your basic technical knowledge. No prior coding experience needed to participate in this event.
Rules
  • Treasure Hack is an individual event and will be conducted in two phases.
  • Every participant must register in eprozyne2k18 website by paying registration amount of INR 300/-
  • First phase is Pre-phase will start on 28-02-2018 at 10:00 PM, which consists of 5 rounds, takes place in eprozyne2k18 website and second phase is the Final phase which will be held on 06-03-2018 in our computer labs.
  • Update - 28-2-18 : pre-phase links are open. Login to play
  • In Pre-phase, each round will consist of a puzzle and correct answer of the puzzle will provide eligibility for next round.
  • Registrations are open until 05-03-18.
  • one puzzle is released everyday at 11:00 PM ,until 05-03-18. participants can attempt all released puzzles upon registration.
  • Participants who had cleared the final round of pre-phase will get a pass key and eligibility for final phase.
  • Participants have to save all correct answers of pre-phase, which may help for solving the final phase.
  • Topics for the puzzle are from engineering domain.
  • Prizes: Cash Prizes worth of INR 5,000/- will be given
