About

EPROZYNE, A National Level Technical symposium is organized by Department of CSE and Department of IT as part of the annual mega technical event of the college "STRIDES". Every year "STRIDES" come with lot of technology, innovation, learning, competition and loads & loads of fun. Besides the regular Techno Talk(paper presentation contest), App Expo, Idea Bucket, Code Tackle, Quizmodo and Treasure Hack, a host of innovative spot events offers a colorful platform for young engineers to showcase their talents.
